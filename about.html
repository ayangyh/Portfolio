A little bit about me.

From a designer of physical spaces to digital spaces, I design for humans.

My passion for creating started in my aunt’s art studio, where I squeezed a slab of modelling clay in my 4-year-old hands. From that moment forward, I dove head first into anything that gave me a blank canvas.

Along the way, graphic design and furniture making led me into the world of blending colours and patterns with the physical container surrounding them. Seeing how people interact with the interior space — what they see, touch, and hear — pushed my curiosity in human behaviours and steered my path to UX design.

Great design stems from learning about our users’ needs and challenges and it’s that journey to finding answers to the “whys” that allows me to recreate stories in delightful experiences with impact.
