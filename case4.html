overview

Our Contact page has not changed since an external agency redesigned the page using an MVP approach. We were tasked to modernize the page while improving the navigational cues for the different types of visitors seeking the support they need.

Goals that guided the process:

  • Reduce the number of users who are filling out the lead generation form by accident
  • Help our general group of website visitors to better self-select and get to the appropriate support funnel

Challenges

A clear path for evolution — Through initial stakeholder interviews and competitor research, it was established that the page needed to define user-specific methods of communication so the users could get the support they need. This was largely based on the fact that the users are introduced to the page with a sales demo form that overtook the entire page. There were confusions around the call-to-action for account support as it was not well-defined.

Our solution was to have a bifurcator as a first step, so that users could identify the call-to-actions based on their needs. We explored the different ways to evolve the designs for the contact cards from exercising a palette expansion of different opacities to playful treatments that echo the illustrations.
