overview

An early-stage design system was in place for the DailyPay that met the fundamental styling requirements for the website. As our team collaborated more with external teams and freelancers, it revealed an opportunity to expand the library. My ambition was to better manage our designs with unity and improve efficiency between cross-functional teams.

Goals that guided the process:

  • Create an easier transition when onboarding an outsourced freelancer or contractor
  • Make our file handoffs to external agencies seamless
  • Align the communication between design and development
